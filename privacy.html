SoBusy Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

SoBusy is a day planner app designed to help you organize your schedule. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

Information Stored by the App

SoBusy stores the information you create in the app, such as events, notes, tags, favorites, reminders, attachments, and planner preferences.

This information is stored locally on your device.

Notifications

If you choose to enable notifications, SoBusy uses notification permissions to send reminders for your events. You can change notification access at any time in your device settings.

Photos and Files

If you choose to attach a photo or file to an event, SoBusy may request access to your photo library or files. This access is only used to let you select and attach content you choose.

Location Search

SoBusy can help you search for event locations using Apple location search services when you enter a place. The app does not require continuous location tracking.

Data Sharing

SoBusy does not sell your personal information and does not use third-party advertising SDKs or third-party analytics SDKs.

Backups

If you export a backup, the backup file is created only when you choose to do so and is saved to the location you select.
