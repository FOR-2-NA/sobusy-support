SoBusy Privacy Policy

SoBusy is a day planner app.

The app stores planner data locally on your device.

If you enable notifications, SoBusy uses notification permissions to send event reminders.

If you choose to attach photos or files to events, the app will request access only for that purpose.

SoBusy does not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

If you have privacy questions, contact carry.method49@icloud.com.
